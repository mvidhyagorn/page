About

I am a computational researcher at the Mahidol Oxford Tropical Medicine Research Unit (MORU) in Bangkok, Thailand. My work focuses on developing and applying mathematical models to understand the dynamics of infectious diseases, particularly in resource-limited settings.

I combine epidemiological theory, statistical methods, and computational techniques to provide insights that can inform public health policy and intervention strategies.
